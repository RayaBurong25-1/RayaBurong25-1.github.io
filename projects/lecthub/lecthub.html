HackMIT 2019: LectHub

github

Who

LectHub is a project made during the HackMIT 2019 event. Our team consists of four team members:

  • Pasapol Saowakon
  • Khemarat Boonyapaluk
  • Arun Wongprommoon
  • Krittamate Tiankanol

What

LectHub is inspired by MIT Open Courseware, and its concept was to democratize lecture videos and resources - to make a Youtube for academia. We decided to add in some features to help facilitate users, for example automatic captioning, one-click realtime caption editing, and support for LaTeX in captions.

Where

This project was done on MIT campus during HackMIT, the university's hackathon.

When

14 September 2020 - 15 September 2020 (24 hours)

Why

We came up with the idea of LectHub during HackMIT because it is something that would help with education. It would also count as assistive technology as well.

How

The frontend was mainly Javascript and HTML, and the backend was mainly Django. I contributed mainly on the frontend in this project, writing the template HTMLs.
