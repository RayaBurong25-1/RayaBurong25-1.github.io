CS999: Experimental Research

github

Who

This project was done in a team of three seniors in Phillips Exeter Academy during the Srping 2019 term. The members are:

  • Gordon Chi
  • Victor Luo
  • Arun Wongprommoon

What

As a final project of CS999 (Machine Learning), we wanted to explore translation in low resource languages (in this case Thai-English and Chinese-English), and see if feeding bigrams instead of words in the word segmentation - word2vec - seq2seq procedure would improve the quality of translation.

Where

This project was done in Phillips Exeter Academy, Exeter, NH.

When

16 May 2019 - 27 May 2020 (2 weeks)

Why

The project itself is a demonstration of our knowledge of machine learning that we learnt throughout the semester, and apply it to a problem of interest. Machine translation, a problem in Natural Language Processing, came to be one of the topics all three of us are passionate about.

How

The project was coded mainly with pytorch and gensim, another NLP Python package.
