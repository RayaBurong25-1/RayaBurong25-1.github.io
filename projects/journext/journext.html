What

Shamelessly copied from the website:

The compact collaborative trip-planning platform, that you've never seen before. Explore places to see, eat, stay and add them together in a schedule!

"JourNext" was made during MIT's web.lab 2020, to address the problem of not having an all-in-one-platform for collaborative travel planning

I'd like to think of this as the greatest mashup of Google Docs, Google Maps and Google Calendar, catered towards the intended audience of group backpackers.

Where

This project was done on MIT campus during the Independent Activities Period, in the cold winter months of Boston.

When

6 January 2020 - 31 January 2020 (26 days)

Why

We came up with the idea of JourNext during the web.lab contest because we thought it's something quite novel, quite challanging to make and is beneficial for at least ourselves and our generation of world explorers.

How

We used the course's template and build on top of it using React.js. The backend was done using MongoDB. I contributed mostly to the frontend of this project, but more importantly, we only learnt how to make a website from scratch within the span of two weeks!
