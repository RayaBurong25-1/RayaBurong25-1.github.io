[001] Transitioning

1 September, 2024

Context: These poems are straight from my journals during the past week. I graduated my M.Eng in May, moved to San Francisco in July, and went on a trip to visit friends in New York and finish moving out from my Boston apartment. I miss college and the environment I was in for the past 5 years.

i. red-eye to boston (8/26/2024)

im back
and it feels like the SF part of my life was a fever dream and the Boston the reality, not the other way around
kinda crying rn lmao, yes it's 7am and nobody's anywhere, but the city gave me a warm welcome back and the charm of the red brick hasn't disappeared
actually everything feels surreal, but im touching the doorknob of the tech office and seeing it with my own eyes, so it must be real right
the work laptop is also real, but is so out of place it's so weird i feel like a time traveller / imposter version of myself / a movie where someone didn't do their research and put something from the future into a period piece

iii. wisdom from new yorkers (8/27/2024)

other people are doing it [adulting] why wouldn't i

iv. windows (8/28/2024)

window to the sun is a great window
also.. not being by [sitting directly next to] the co founders is great

i love the A train

the weird thing is: only my watch and laptop are set to pacific time, but it creates a pretty convincing portal and sometimes i do actually forget what time it actually is outside

the discrepancy between the portal and the surroundings make it a bit hard to enjoy both realities

v. boston the comfort zone (8/28/2024)

boston remains my comfort zone
i get the same feeling when i get here as when i go home in bangkok
it's where i still feel belonging
but slowly i am coming to the reality
as i walk past groups of terrascope frosh 2028s
that im getting too old for this city
and time is kicking me out to uproot to san francisco

i retrace the steps from kendall to my (old) apartment
some bits are changing, becoming a bit unfamiliar
like the outbound exit now white wall and attached to the google building
or the weeds growing taller
boston is not just a place, it was a time and a people
the apartment? only a shell of us, its former inhabitants, time is running out before the new tenants push us out

i awkwardly pull the boston apartment keys from deep inside my wallet
thinking as i unlock it that im such an imposter now
the kitchen floor is redone
and the bathroom looks strange even though it looks the same
i still connect to the wifi immediately, but it's getting harder for me to connect with the house again

why am i just pacing in the apartment

old and crusty >>> new and fancy

vi. the college pace (8/29/2024)

lunch with [a college friend] reminds me of the college pace

[i need to be] happy being with myself

you know another thing that i miss
is how young and diverse the environment feels, and that not everybody is doing tech

the youthful energy and the diversity of knowledge and aspirations in the environment is so refreshing.. nobody's just building a startup or amassing money

vii. joy and energy (8/30/2024)

work, social, self: gotta find a good balance

throwing mattresses is actually the most fun thing i've done in a while

reuniting with the boston roommates!
it's so refreshing to be back and change up the environment

going into stud post activities midway is something else
the ENERGY and the passion
maybe sf is just devoid of passion
and the proximity
just running into [a college friend] and a few other people is just a wonderful surprise

i feel so split personality right now one im still working the other i want to enjoy boston

viii. wake-up call (8/31/2024)

wake up
reality is not here it's there
and i have to fight
for my work for social life and for myself

it's my last night here
but im not overly sad or anything
maybe im starting to accept the fact that
i no longer should be here
my mind should no longer linger in this city
or maybe it's just
i will come back in three weeks

ix. alas (9/1/2024)

alas
i'm back in san francisco
it only gets better from here I guess
it's incredibly sunny out here
but wow am i suddenly depressed
isn't that crazy
this city is the mundane
one track minded tech startup bros

lol once i landed in sf
i immediately feel depressed
almost as if there's a conservation of clouds
boston has clouds in the sky
the overly bright sunny california weather puts clouds in my head
